New Business Profit Prediction

Insurance products were bought by our customers and it used to take 15-30 days to access the value of the policy. The problem statement was to reduce the tat such that investments and finance teams can make timely decisions.

For training, I used historic policy level data with global indicators such as govt. bond yields for 1, 5, 30 years. The policy data had customer demographics along with the product information and buy price to train a product agnostic regression model for predicting new business value.
